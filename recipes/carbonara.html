Carbonara

A spaghetti dish, with some sort of salted pork product and an egg sauce.

Quick, simple and tasty.

  • Eggs Bacon (Or some other cured pork product) Spaghetti
  1. Cook the pasta according to the instructions on the box Slightly brown the bacon Add the bacon to the spaghetti, and take the pan off the heat Add the egg when the pan is off the heat, so the sauce doesnt break
